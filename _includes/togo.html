Wearable Travel Games

Overview

To Go Games was a set of travel friendly games and accessories developed for my senior thesis project. Our class’ thesis theme was ‘game design’ and after the research portion of the project, I decided to focus on portable games for travel. I wanted to create analog games that were social, space conscience, and had flexible game play.

In 2020, I took photos of my senior project and finished the branding document to be more inline with the positioning documents I had created.
